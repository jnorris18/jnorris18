TIMELINE OF KOBE'S CAREER
  • 1996 - Vlade Divac is traded to Philidelphia for 17 year old Kobe Bryant
  • 1997 - In his first season Kobe gains fans by winning the Slam Dunk Contest
  • 1998 - Becomes youngest NBA All-Star starter
  • 2000 - Pulls in double double with 26 points 10 rebounds to win game 6 of the NBA finals against the Pacers to win first championship
  • 2001 - Becomes top voted for NBA All-Star game
  • 2002 - Drops 30 in game 7 of NBA finals against the Kings, and Lakers win their thrid consecutive title
  • 2003 - Bryant is charged with sexual assault
  • 2004 - Resigns with Lakers after many uncertainties
  • 2005 - Wins leagues leading scorer award with 27.6 points per game
  • 2006 - Becomes 2nd leading scorer(in one game) after scoring 81 points at a home game against the Raptors
  • 2007 - Wins another scoring title with 31.6 points per game
  • 2008 - Wins first and only MVP Award
  • 2008 - Wins first Gold olympic medal
  • 2010 - Scores 44 against the Grizzlies and passes Jerry West for all time leading scorer for Lakers
  • 2010 - Lakers beat celtics in Game 7 of NBA finals for Kobe's fifth ring
  • 2013 - 15th All-Star game apperance, and passes Wilt Chamberlain for fourth in all time scoring
  • 2014 - Takes his spot as thrid all time scorer
  • 2016 - Takes his fair-well tour faceing each team for the last time before retirement

"Everything negative - pressure, challenges - is all an opportunity for me to rise." -Kobe Bryant
